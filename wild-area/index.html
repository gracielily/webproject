Welcome to a brand new world of Pokémon

Pokémon Sword & Shield is a game on Nintendo Switch that introduces the region of Galar, an expansive land of dynamic cities, rolling countryside, forest and mountains. Players can visit Gyms throughout Galar as they train to be the Champion.

Choose Your Partner

Choose a new Pokémon to be your first partner in Pokémon Sword for Switch:

  • Grookey, a Grass-type Chimp Pokémon, is mischievous and curious
  • Scorbunny, a Fire-type Rabbit Pokémon, is bursting with energy
  • Sobble, a Water-type Water Lizard Pokémon who shoots out stealth attacks from the water.

Features of Pokémon Sword & Shield

  • Explore the newly discovered Galar region
  • Idyllic countryside and contemporary cities
  • Visit Gyms in your quest to become Champion
  • Search for never-before-seen Pokémon in the Wild Area
  • Team up to take on wild Dynamax Pokémon in Max Raid Battles
  • See Pokémon take on gigantic appearances in battle
