What is the Galar Region?

The Galar Region is the setting for the games. When creating the game, the creators based the region on real world locations across the United Kingdom. The game draws cues from the locations, culture, and history of the UK to create a land full of places to explore.

Lots to Explore

During your adventure, you will be traveling through the Galar region, where you will find many unique locations, such as huge grassy plains filled with nature and towns and cities where people and Pok√©mon work and live together!
