A Steam Powered Marvel

The city of Motostoke is based on Manchester, a British city with a long industrial history. Motostoke is full of Weezing that filter the air and makes it free of toxins.

Motostoke Stadium

Motostoke Stadium is where you battle Kabu - the Fire Type gym leader. His uniform is based on the uniform worn by the football team Manchester United.
