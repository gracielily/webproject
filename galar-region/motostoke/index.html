Motostoke

Cental Galar

A Steam Powered Marvel

Mototstoke is a city located in the centre of the galar region. It is known across the region of Galar as an industrial city filled with marvels of modern engineering. It is a place where people and pokemon work hard to develop the Galar region together.

Motostoke is split into two different levels. The player can travel between the two levels using big, wheel-shaped elevators.
