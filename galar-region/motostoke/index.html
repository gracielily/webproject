An Industrial City full of the Wonders of Modern Engineering

Mototstoke is a city located in the centre of the galar region. It is known across the region of Galar as an industrial city filled with marvels of modern engineering. It is a place where people and Pokémon work hard to develop the Galar region together.


Motostoke is split into two different levels. The player can travel between the two levels using big, wheel-shaped elevators.

A Steam Powered Marvel

The city of Motostoke is based on Manchester, a British city with a long industrial history. Motostoke is full of Weezing that filter the air and makes it free of toxins.

Motostoke Stadium

Motostoke Stadium is at the heart of Motostoke. It is here where you will battle Kabu - the no nonsense Fire Type gym leader. The uniform that he wears is based on an iteration of the uniform worn by the football team Manchester United.
